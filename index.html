INTRODUCTION

L&T Resources is a newly establish labour contract, recruitment & engineering company, based in Rengit, Johor Darul Takzim. Incorporated in September 2013, L&T Resources is striving hard to meets the customer demands, to upkeep and upgrading its works quality and slowly moving into oil and gas industries. Establish by Mr. Kenny Loo where he holds the reins and spearheading the organization towards its goals. Mr. Kenny Loo is a former JGC Corporation Piping Superintendent, stationed in Algeria Centralized Facilities Processing Unit with his project completion in 2011. He had more than 25 years’ experience in oil and gas industries especially fabrication and installation of :-

* Piping Fabrication & Installation
* Fabrication of Pressure Vessels & Metering Skid
* Siphonic Rainwater Downpipe System (UPVC) Fabrication & Installation
* Steel Structural Fabrication & Installation
* Hull Fitting Fabrication & Installation
* Recruitment of Technical Personnel
* Manpower Services for Skill Personnel

L&T Resources in its attributes to its success, enduring customer relationship in varied business industries while being supported by strong and skillful staff which bring to a strong commitment to customer’s satisfaction, innovative thinking and total quality where L&T Resources is poised to meets its challenges and the needs of its customer. L&T Resources is ready to provide its services in Engineering & Construction works where include the following activities.

* Fabrication & Erection Works
* Plant Maintenance Services
* Turnaround Works
* Supply of Technical & Skill Personnel
* Project Recruitment

Our Policy

QUALITY CONTROL POLICY

The Quality & Reliability of L&T Resources are concern of everyone in the organization and not only just the sole responsibilities of the Quality Assurance. The prosperity of the Company can only be assured is by the continued satisfaction of its customer.

L&T Resources requires all staff and employees to accept their shares of responsibilities for the quality and reputation of the Company. To achieved, the Company had to maintain by continuing programmed of quality reliability improvement where hence essential elements of its objectives.

L&T Resources except parts of its operating philosophy the fact that Quality must be built in at all levels within the organization structure in the aims to provide products and services to the satisfy standard and the requirements of our customer.

L&T Resources adopt international management standard in high emphasis on quality in effort to achieve perfect results through: -

* There shall be no compromise where Quality and performance of our services and products are concerned.
* Quality is the responsibilities of every employee and this policy shall be understood, implemented and maintained at all levels.
* Quality is our goal and shall strive for continuous improvement from time to time. 

SAFETY, HEALTH & ENVIRONMENT POLICY

It’s the policy of L&T Resources that our activities shall be conducted in a manner, which bring safeguards in the Safety, Health & Welfare of all people and preserves the natural Environment, as far as, practicable.
Our Safety, Health & Environment objective shall bear importance with our fundamental business objectives. In pursuance of this policy and adherence to all legislative requirements, L&T Resources is hereby committed to: -
* Prevent all accidents, occupational disease and fire.
* Prevent damage to plant, equipment and property.
* Preserve the natural environment.
* Implement safe systems of works.
* Provide adequate information, instruction, training and supervision.
* Promote safety consciousness and safety awareness.
* Safeguards the interest of the general public and surrounding community.
* Comply with all applicable rules and regulation of the industries.
* Respond quickly and effectively to the emergencies, accidents and incident resulting from its operation.
